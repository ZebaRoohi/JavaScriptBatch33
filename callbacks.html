Callback ,Callback Hell

Callback: a callback is a function passed as an argument to another function. The purpose of a callback is to be executed later, after the completion of an asynchronous operation or a certain task. Callbacks are commonly used in event handling, AJAX requests,

Callback hell, also known as the "Pyramid of Doom," occurs when you have multiple nested callbacks, making the code hard to read and maintain. This often happens in asynchronous JavaScript, especially when dealing with nested callbacks for I/O operations.
